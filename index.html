NFL Match Prediction

I developed an NFL match prediction algorithm for over two years with a team of four at Northeastern University's NUCAR. Our algorithm was able to predict 55% of matches correctly, greater than the 53% needed to profit. My role on the team was widespread, but I solely developed the dimensionality reduction and model selection tools. For dimensionality reduction, we used cross-validation in order to quickly determine the most significant features. In order to prevent overfitting with the models, we would use a validation set of data along with a training and test set.
